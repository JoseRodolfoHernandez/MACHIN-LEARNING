Use Case 3: Customer Purchase Prediction

Problem

Businesses want to know if a customer is likely to make a purchase based on their behavior and personal data.

Solution with Machine Learning

A classification model (logistic regression) predicts whether a customer will buy or not.

How does it work?

  • 1. Customer data is collected (age, income, visits).
  • 2. The data is labeled (buy / not buy).
  • 3. A logistic regression model is trained.
  • 4. The model predicts customer behavior.

Benefits

  • Personalized marketing strategies.
  • Higher conversion rates.
  • Better customer segmentation.
