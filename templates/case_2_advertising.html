Use Case 2: Advertising Impact on Sales

Problem

Companies invest money in advertising but do not always know how it affects their sales performance.

Solution with Machine Learning

A regression model analyzes the relationship between advertising investment and sales to predict expected revenue.

How does it work?

  • 1. Advertising data is collected (TV, radio, online).
  • 2. Sales data is analyzed.
  • 3. A linear regression model is trained.
  • 4. The model predicts sales based on advertising budget.

Benefits

  • Optimized marketing investment.
  • Better decision-making.
  • Increased return on investment (ROI).
