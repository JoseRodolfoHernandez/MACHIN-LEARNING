Use Case 1: Sales Prediction

Problem

Companies do not always know how much they will sell in the future. This can lead to overstock (excess inventory) or shortages.

Solution with Machine Learning

A machine learning model analyzes historical sales data to predict future sales and improve business planning.

How does it work?

  • 1. Historical sales data is collected.
  • 2. A regression model is trained.
  • 3. The model identifies patterns in sales.
  • 4. Future sales are predicted.

Benefits

  • Better inventory management.
  • Improved financial planning.
  • Reduced losses.
