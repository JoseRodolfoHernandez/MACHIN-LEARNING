Use Case: Machine Learning in Sales and Stock

Problem

Companies do not always know how much they will sell in the future. This can lead to overstock (excess inventory) or shortages.

Solution with Machine Learning

A machine learning model is implemented that analyzes historical sales data, dates, seasons, and customer behavior to predict how many units of a product will be sold in the next period.

How does it work?

  • 1. Historical sales data is collected.
  • 2. A regression model is trained.
  • 3. The model learns demand patterns.
  • 4. The system automatically predicts future sales.

Benefits

  • Reduction of losses due to excess inventory.
  • better purchasing planning.
  • Cash flow optimization.
  • Decisions based on data.

Technologies Used

Python, Flask y Scikit-learn.
