Logistic Regression - Marketing Analytics

Problem Description

The objective is to predict whether a customer will purchase a product based on their behavior.

Type of Machine Learning

Supervised Learning - Classification

Dataset

The dataset includes features such as age, income, website visits, time spent on site, previous purchases, and discounts used.

Variables

  • Inputs: Age, Income, Visits, Time, Purchases, Discount
  • Output: Purchase (0 = No, 1 = Yes)

Model Explanation

Logistic regression predicts the probability of a binary outcome using a sigmoid function.

Example

A customer who visits frequently and has high income has a higher probability of buying.
