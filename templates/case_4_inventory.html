Use Case 4: Inventory Optimization

Problem

Managing inventory is complex, and poor planning can lead to excess stock or product shortages.

Solution with Machine Learning

A regression model predicts product demand based on historical sales and trends.

How does it work?

  • 1. Historical demand data is collected.
  • 2. Sales trends are analyzed.
  • 3. A predictive model is trained.
  • 4. The system recommends stock levels.

Benefits

  • Reduced inventory costs.
  • Improved supply chain efficiency.
  • Better product availability.
